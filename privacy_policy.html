Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Safe Roads Chennai. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register for an account and report road issues:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates to accurately pinpoint the location of reported road issues.
  • Media: Photos of road damage or safety hazards you choose to upload.
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and app usage statistics.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used specifically to enhance road safety in Chennai:

  • To facilitate the reporting and tracking of road-related complaints.
  • To notify you of updates regarding the status of your reports.
  • To help authorities identify and prioritize road maintenance tasks.
  • To improve our application's performance and user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with authorized government bodies and road maintenance departments in Chennai specifically for the purpose of resolving reported issues.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time within the app settings. You can also contact our support team for any data-related queries.
